Game Rules

Matchup Round Game Scoring Description
T1 vs T2 1st - 9 Added Score 1 point per hole Every player plays own ball. Added scores of partners scores. P1+P2=X - Ex. 4+5=9
T3 vs T4 1st - 9 Own Ball Tie 1/2 point each Every player plays own ball.
T1 vs T3 2nd - 9 Best Score (Match) 1 point per hole Every player plays own ball, best score between partners counts as hole score.
T2 vs T4 2nd - 9 Own Ball Tie 1/2 point each Every player plays own ball.
T1 vs T4 3rd - 9 Modified Vegas 1 point per hole Every player plays own ball, score combined. P1 + P2 = P1,P2 - Ex. 4 + 5 = 45
T2 vs T3 3rd - 9 Own Ball Tie 1/2 point each Every player plays own ball.
1st vs 4th 4th - 9 2-man Best Ball 1 point per hole 2-man best ball in teams.
2nd vs 3rd 4th - 9 Best Ball Tie 1/2 point each Seeded based on standings of first 3 rounds.
